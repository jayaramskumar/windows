Aristotle Onansis

One of the richest people in the 20th century

Aristotle Socrates Onansis(Greek:Aptatotenic vaons,Aristotelis Onasis: 20 January 1906-15 March 1975).
Commonly called Ari or Ari or Aristo Onasis was a Greek-Argentine shopping magnate,who amassed the world's
largest privately owned shipping shipping fleet and was one of the world's richest and famous men.
